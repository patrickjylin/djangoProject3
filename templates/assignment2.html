A Quick Explainer on Ballroom Dance

There are 4 main styles in competitive ballroom dance,
with different dances in each style

International Standard

  • Waltz
  • Tango
  • Viennese Waltz
  • Foxtrot
  • Quickstep

International Latin

  • Chacha
  • Samba
  • Rumba
  • Paso Doble
  • Jive

American Smooth

  • Waltz
  • Tango
  • Foxtrot
  • Viennese Waltz

American Rhythm

  • Chacha
  • Rumba
  • Swing
  • Bolero
  • Mambo

Each round consists of competitors dancing all 4 or 5 dances in a given style. Each dance lasts for one minute and 30 seconds. An example round and standard timing of songs for the International Standard style is shown below:

Dance Duration Tempo
Waltz 1:30 84 BPM
Tango 1:30 128 BPM
Viennese Waltz 1:30 170 BPM
Foxtrot 1:30 114 BPM
Quickstep 1:30 200 BPM
